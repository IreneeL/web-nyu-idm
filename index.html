The story of my name

I used to use my Chinese name in schools.

Then one day,

I was told by my mother to choose a English name for myself before going into high school so I can fit in with others.

I don't know what to do.

So I googled names for girls and wrote down a list of names and gave it to my friends to choose the one that suits me.

And eventually that is how I got my English name.

btw the name is Irene :)
